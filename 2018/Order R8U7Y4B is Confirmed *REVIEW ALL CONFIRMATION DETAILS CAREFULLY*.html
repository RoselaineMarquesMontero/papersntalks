When booking transportation please consider the travel time and book an earlier shuttle to avoid missing your flight. DOTS is not responsible for missed flights or missed shuttles.

DeLand and Deltona pick up locations are are typically 25 mins/35 mins after the stated confirmation times for the TO MCO/SFB run and 60-70 mins after the FROM MCO/SFB stated times.

ERAU and BCU campus pick ups must be ready 1 hour prior to confirmation time. Pick Up fee is included in the Student fare. We may schedule these picks prior to or en route from the stated shuttle time pending day to day circumstance. (Student will be contacted day of to confirm)

When travelling from the airport please allow yourself ample time to collect baggage and reach our shuttle. We generally suggest 45 to 60 minutes after your scheduled arrival for domestic flights from MCO and less time for flights from SFB or DAB.

At the Orlando Airport DOTS departs from terminal A & B, space 14/ 15 on the ground transportation level (level 1). Ground transportation is located one level below the baggage claim. Be aware that our shuttle departs from Terminal A at the time indicated on your reservation. The shuttle arrives on Terminal B up to 15 minutes EARLIER.

At Sanford airport DOTS departs from within the shuttle and taxi waiting area directly across the street from the B terminal.

At Daytona Airport DOTS departs from past the taxi area adjacent to rental car return lot outside the glass doors past baggage claim. (follow signs to ground transportation)

Please contact DOTS at 1-386-257-5411 ext. 1 in the event that your flight or baggage is delayed.

Home/Offsite Pickup/Drop Offs Available! (additional charges based on location) Book online or contact DOTS at 1-386-257-5411 ext 2, prior to travel to schedule a pickup. All pickups must be scheduled by 2:00 pm on the day prior to travel. Have your reservation number ready and we will be happy to assist you. Pick ups are typically scheduled 1 hour prior to stated Daytona terminal departure time.
